Frequently asked

Is it really offline?

Yes. All words, questions, explanations, and audio are bundled in the app. Nothing you do is sent to a server.

How does the purchase work?

You can try every section for free. A single one-time purchase unlocks the full app — no subscription, no ads. Use “Restore Purchases” to re-unlock on a new device with the same Apple ID.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to the paywall or Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. It uses Apple’s standard mechanism.

Where is my progress stored?

On your device only. Deleting the app removes it. There is no account to sign into.

Are these real exam questions?

No. Every question is original, written to match the real GRE format and difficulty. We do not reproduce copyrighted exam material.
