FAQ

Does GRE Peanut work offline?

Yes. All content and study help run on your device. The only network connection the app ever makes is to Apple's App Store, when you buy or restore the one-time purchase.

How do the trial and the purchase work?

Every section is free to try for three days. After that, a single one-time In-App Purchase unlocks the full app permanently. There is no subscription. Vocabulary practice stays free even after the trial.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore purchase". Restoring uses your Apple ID, so it works on any iPhone signed in to the same Apple ID.

Is the practice score my real GRE score?

No. Mock exams give a practice estimate to track your progress; it is not an official GRE score.
