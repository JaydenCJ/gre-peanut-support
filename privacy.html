Privacy Policy — GRE Peanut

Last updated: June 3, 2026

GRE Peanut ("the app") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Information we collect

The app has no account system, no analytics, and no servers. We do not sell or share your data.

All of your study data — your goal score, exam date, progress, streaks, mastered words, drafts, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

On-device study help (Peanut AI)

Peanut AI — the in-app study coach, question explanations, essay feedback, and chat helper — runs entirely on your device. Its explanations are generated on-device from the original study content bundled inside the app. It makes no network connection, connects to no AI service, and sends nothing you type to any server. Your messages, drafts, and question context never leave your iPhone.

Purchases

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full version. Payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store — the only time the app touches the network. We do not see or receive your payment details. Restoring a purchase uses Apple's standard mechanism.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not send your study data, drafts, or chats off the device.

Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.
