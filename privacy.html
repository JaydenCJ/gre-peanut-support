Privacy Policy

GRE Peanut · Last updated May 31, 2026

GRE Peanut (“the app”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Information we collect

None on our servers. The app has no account system and no analytics. We do not operate a server that collects your data.

All of your study data — goal score, exam date, progress, streaks, mastered words, drafts, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Purchases

The app offers a one-time purchase to unlock the full version. Payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or receive your payment details. Restoring a purchase uses Apple’s standard mechanism.

Offline by design

The app does not connect to any server of ours. All practice content is bundled in the app, and all explanations are written in advance. There is no live AI service, and nothing you type is sent anywhere.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

Children’s privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new “Last updated” date.
